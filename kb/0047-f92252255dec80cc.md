# Eid al-Adha 2020 - Cambourne Crescent

Friday 31st July has been confirmed as the day of Eid al-Adha In’sha Allah.

Eid al-Adha

First Jama’at

Arrival 8:00

Jama’at 8:15

Finish by 9:00

Second Jama’at

Arrival 9:15

Jama’at 9:30

Finish by 10:00

Jum’mah

Arrival 1:15

Khutbah 1:30

Guidance for the safe use of places of worship

- Arrive on time

- Must present the e-mail confirmation, electronic or printed, for the entry

- Sanitise hands, on arrival & departure

- Social distance 2M

- Bring own prayer mat

- Bring shoe bag

- Wear a mask

- No children under age 12

- Pray at home if unwell

- Pray at home if over 70 or vulnerable

- No wudhu or toilet at the HUB

- Please kindly follow instructions from volunteers at all times.

- Between the two Adhans you may pray any Sunnah prayers, but not afterwards.

- To exit safely and to maintain social distancing, after the prayer you must remain seated until it is your turn to leave.

- Worshippers will exit one row at a time, under the direction of volunteers.
