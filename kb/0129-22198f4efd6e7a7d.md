# About us - Cambourne Crescent

About us

Cambourne Crescent is a local Islamic Charitable Trust established in 2011. Based in Cambourne, Cambridge, UK. It is registered with the Charity Commission under Registration Number: 1174750.

The Trust is managed by a group of volunteers who are practising professionals from a diverse range of backgrounds.

Cambourne Crescent was established to fulfil the social and spiritual needs of families living in and around Cambourne Village and to engage with and actively contribute and participate in the wider community of Cambourne.
