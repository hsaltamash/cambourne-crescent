# Ladies Tea and Coffee evening - Cambourne Crescent

On Friday 16th March Cambourne Ladies will be celebrating together with party games over a cuppa. Due to popular demand, this has become a regular event and it’s held once a month on a Friday from 8 – 10.30 pm. This is an open event and we look forward to seeing many of you there on Friday 16th at the Cambourne Hub.
