# Precautionary Measures for COVID – 19 - Cambourne Crescent

“If you get wind of the outbreak of plague in a land, do not enter it; and if it breaks out in a land in which you are, do not leave it.”

– Prophet (ﷺ)

Cambourne Crescent Schools and Clubs

In light of the recent outbreak of COVID-19 (Coronavirus), we will be following the government guidelines until further notice.

We request all the teachers and parents/students to follow below recommendations –

- If you have arrived from a COVID-19 Specified Country in the last 14-days, to make it safe for others please self-isolate, and do not attend CC school.

www.gov.uk/government/publications/covid-19-specified-countries-and-areas/covid-19-specified-countries-and-areas-with-implications-for-returning-travellers-or-visitors-arriving-in-the-uk - If you are experiencing symptoms of COVID-19 (cough, difficulty in breathing and/or fever) please do not attend any of the CC school and follow guidance on self-isolation.

- Please follow hand-washing protocols regularly throughout the day.

If the situation changes and we decide to close the school, we will alert all the parents and teachers via email and social media.

Corona Virus advice to the Public

Stay aware of the latest information on the COVID-19 outbreak. Most people who become infected experience mild illness and recover, but it can be more severe for others.

Misinformation about pandemics can cause somebody’s life. Stay informed and do not fall prey to fake news. Take care of your health and protect others.

Coronavirus advise from reliable sources –

NHS: www.nhs.uk/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/

World Health Organization (WHO): www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019/advice-for-public
