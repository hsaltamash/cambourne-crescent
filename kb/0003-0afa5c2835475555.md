# New Cambourne Crescent website launched - Cambourne Crescent

After several months of selfless hard work by someone who doesn’t want any limelight, the new look and feel website is here. We respect and appreciate the hard work that was put in running the website for last 8 years and we pray to Allah Subhanahu Wa Ta’ala for his and his families well being.

The website has been rebuilt and now it’s mobile friendly providing an interactive experience with all the relevant information in an easy to find format.

For example, the daily and monthly Salah time for Cambourne is easier to look up and by clicking on the venue opens up the location in google maps –

www.cambournecrescent.org/prayer

(remember to bookmark, or add to home-screen on your phone)

To ensure being informed, subscribe to the newsletter and email service through the website. Also depending on preferences, follow the facebook and twitter feeds.
