# Cambourne VTF - Food Donations - Cambourne Crescent

We have been doing FoodBank collection for last few years now for Cambridge City Foodbank (CCFB) and the Trussell Trust network, along with Cambourne Church.

The Cambourne’s Volunteer Task Force (COVID-19) is collecting for local elderly people, people in self-isolation and people concerned about going out by donating items from the list below.

? Venue: Blue School

? Time: 6 – 8 pm Monday 16th March

The most needed items are:

- Canned food: vegetables, fruits, soups, tuna, meat, pasta sauce, …

- Dry food: rice, pasta, beans, cereal, chips, snacks, biscuits…

- Drinks: UHT milk, coffee, tea

- Cooking oil

- Handwash liquid, soap, toilet paper, tissues, dish soap, laundry detergent, etc.

- Baby Food and Nappies

- Pet supplies

* ALL GOODS SHOULD BE UNDAMAGED PLEASE & IF POSSIBLE HAVE LONG BBE DATES.

If you need HELP, please do let us know – www.CambourneCrescent.org/vtf
