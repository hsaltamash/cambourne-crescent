# Islamic Wills Workshop - Cambourne Crescent

Some of the questions we will be explore together, In’sha Allah:

- Why do I need a Will?

- What happens if I die without a Will?

- How to minimise Inheritance Tax

- What is the Islamic Law of Inheritance?

- How do you make a Islamic Wills that complies with the Islamic law of Inheritance & English Law?

Venue: Cambourne HUB

Date & Time: Saturday 30th September, 6 pm

Please note, the event will be followed by Bring & Share Dinner and Seerah Competition, details to follow.
