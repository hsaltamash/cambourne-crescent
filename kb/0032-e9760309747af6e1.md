# 1st Cambourne Community of Professionals - Cambourne Crescent

We are planning to arrange a professional networking event for those who live in and around Cambourne. The purpose of this group is to –

- Meet and network with each other and where possible help and share ideas.

- Show case our community’s great talent to inspire and engage our younger generation to achieve great things in life.

- Helping and guidance in professional and social life

- Get to know each other well and socialise

Please fill the form here, if this interests you – https://forms.gle/mytrE9Hosy773khu6

This form is to measure interest in this event so that we can decide how do we move forward.
