# Cambourne Volunteers Fair - Cambourne Crescent

On Saturday 18th November 2017 Cambourne Parish Council will host its second Volunteers Fair in the HUB between 10:00 am and 12:00 pm. The event, held for the first time last year, seeks to match up potential volunteers with Cambourne’s community groups and other volunteer roles.

There are a wide variety of opportunities to get involved in the community. This ranges from helping out once a year for a couple of hours at the Cambourne Community Fete to a regular weekly commitment to a youth organisation and many in between.

It will be a good opportunity to be inclusive in the activities.

Cambourne Crescent is participating in the volunteer’s fair. Please do come along and see how you can get involved and make a difference in the community, feel free to bring something to share and show our cultural hospitality to the community in Cambourne, and enjoy a cuppa with locals.
