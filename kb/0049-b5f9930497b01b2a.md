# The Duke of Edinburgh volunteering - Cambourne Crescent

Cambourne Crescent (CVTF) along with the Cambourne Town Council is providing an opportunity to young students to gain volunteering experience for community work, required for The Duke of Edinburgh award.

The DofE volunteers along with CVTF volunteers have been packing and delivering hampers for key workers (school staff and NHS) as part of this.

If anyone is interested, please contact us.
