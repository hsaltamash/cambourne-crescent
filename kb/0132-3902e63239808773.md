# Volunteers - Cambourne Crescent

Volunteers

For the Sake of Allāh ﷻ

At Cambourne Crescent, volunteering is rooted in our faith — an act done purely for the sake of Allāh ﷻ.

Islam teaches us the virtue of service, compassion, and selflessness.

The Prophet ﷺ said:

“The most beloved of people to Allāh are those who are most beneficial to others.”

(Sunan Ibn Majah 79)

Our volunteers embody this spirit by serving the community with sincerity and purpose.

People of all ages — men and women alike — contribute their time and skills, helping to build a stronger, caring, and empowered community.

At Cambourne Crescent, empowerment is at the heart of everything we do. The Trustees play a facilitative role — enabling, supporting, and guiding — while volunteers take the lead, managing projects and initiatives independently for the sake of Allāh.

Examples of Our Volunteer-Led Model

Cambourne Volunteers Task Force (COVID-19 Response):

During the pandemic, we established the Cambourne Volunteers Task Force — a powerful example of community collaboration.

Under this model, we had a Steering Committee and several active groups including:

-

NHS Support Group

-

Food Delivery Team

-

Pharmacy and Grocery Delivery

-

Meals & Food Parcel Distribution

-

Phone Support Team

Through these efforts, we united Cambourne’s diverse community to serve and protect one another during a time of great need.
