# Welcome To Cambourne - Cambourne Crescent

General

Cambourne is a new settlement in Cambridgeshire, England, in the district of South Cambridgeshire, comprising the villages of Lower, Great, Upper and West Cambourne.

Schools

Cambourne Village College is the only Secondary school in Cambourne

There are 4 primary schools in Cambourne:

- Jeavons Wood Primary School

- Hardwick & Cambourne Community Primary School: Cambourne Campus

- Monkfield Park Primary School

- The Vine Inter-Church Primary School

Surgery

Monlfield Medical Practice is the only surgery in Cambourne, to register see the details here.

For New Muslims in Cambourne

Salah

We pray Jum’ah, Taraweeh, Fajr and Isha in congregation. During summer months we also pray Maghrib in congregation. Since we do not have a permanent place, we hire community spaces (usually NCP, Blue Space and Hub) from the Town Council for the congregations.

Prayer Times and Venues are on the daily/monthly calendar.

Crescent School

- About & Admission form: www.cambournecrescent.org/service/school

- Students are taught Quran and Islamic Studies

- Students are taught how to read, write and speak modern standard Arabic.

- Term time on Sunday from 10:00 am to 2:00 pm in CVC

- Also runs a pre-GCSE class for Arabic to help students with the exams

- We also have classes for adults who are interested in learning the language

Please note that,

- the child must be in year 1 to start the school

- filling the form does not guarantee an admission, places will be subject to availability
