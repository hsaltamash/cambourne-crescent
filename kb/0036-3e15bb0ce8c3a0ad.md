# Cambourne Community of Professionals - 'Start-ups' - Cambourne Crescent

Cambourne Community of Professionals meeting this Sunday at New Cricket Pavilion, Great Cambourne, from 6 to 8 pm.

Agenda:

Shakeel Rashid, an experienced business professional in different businesses and start-ups, will be sharing his experience about ‘Start-ups‘ :

- Do’s and Don’ts

- Business Plan

- Fund Raising

- Strategies

- Due Diligence

- SWOT Analysis

- Company Structure

- Q&A

This is a great opportunity if you want to start a business, already in the business, thinking of a business idea or just curious to learn more about the world of business.

Important: Please arrive at 5:45 pm (sharp) so that we can utilise our time appropriately.
