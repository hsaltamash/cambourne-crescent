# The Life of the Prophet (ﷺ) Competition & Dinner - Cambourne Crescent

It is the month of Rabīʽ al-Awwal, the third month of the Islamic calendar, an important month for us Muslims, as very important events took place during this month mainly the birth and the death of our beloved Prophet Muhammed (ﷺ) and also the Hijrah from Makkah to Madinah.

To celebrate the Seerah and the life of the prophet, we are inviting all of our children aged 4 and above who want to learn more about our beloved prophet and to know him better to participate in.

💚 The Life of the Prophet (ﷺ) competition 🌙

~ organized by Sheikh Ali Omar

🕕 Sunday, Oct 24th, 5-10 pm

📍 Cambourne HUB

🔸 Questions & Answers (see here) will be shared in advance and the children need to prepare them.

🔸 Children aged 4 and above can participate

🔸 Children aged 12 and above can also participate and they will need to prepare a 3 minutes talk about Prophet Muhammed (ﷺ) in addition to preparing the questions
