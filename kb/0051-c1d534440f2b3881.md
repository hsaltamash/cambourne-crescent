# Seerah of the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) Meccan period 🕋︎ - Cambourne Crescent

The Seerah of the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) – Meccan period, course will start from Thursday 28th January, and will be held online every Thursday from 6.30 pm to 7.10 pm. This will be a 10 Week online course by Sheikh Ali Omar.

About the Course

The course is suitable for secondary school children, teenagers and adults. We strongly encourage the whole family to join in.

About the Teacher

Sheikh Ali Omar has a degree in Islamic Studies and Arabic Language from University of Cairo, and has an Ijzah in six narrations of qiraat including Hafs and Qaloon, and a Diploma in Qiraat from Al Azhar University – Cairo. He also has a qualification as a teacher of Islamic Studies from UAE. He further studied at Newman University in Birmingham.
