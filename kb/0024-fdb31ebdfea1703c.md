# Ladies-Only Swimming Lessons at Abbey Pool - Cambourne Crescent

Cambridge Islamic College in association with Abbey Leisure Centre (Better Gym and Pools) has organised beginner swimming lessons for ladies taught by an ASA qualified and well experienced female swimming instructor. It is starting on Friday 1st of March 2019 at Whitehill Road, Cambridge CB5 8NT. This is a wonderful opportunity for those wanting to learn swimming but could not due to lack of facilities.

There are two 30-minute sessions to choose from on Fridays at 7.30pm and 8.00pm during the women-only swimming time at Abbey Leisure Centre. The lessons are charged £28.35 per month (covering 4-5 lessons). This also includes swimming pool entry fees (usually £4.70 per session or about £20 a month) giving very good value.

Places are strictly limited. Please book with Better/Abbey directly by calling 01223 213352 or online at –
