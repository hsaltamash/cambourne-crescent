# Accounts - Cambourne Crescent

Cambourne Crescent bank balance (General Bank Account) since 2012 is detailed below.

We strive to maintain transparency in all our matters including finances (and so always prefer electronic transactions for record keeping).

Our main sources of income are –

- Juma’ah (Friday) collections

- Arabic Language School fee

- Islamic School fee

- Regular donations / Sadaqah

Our main expenditures are –

- Venue bookings

Daily prayer and Juma’ah (Friday) prayer

Ramadan Taraweeh prayer

Eid prayer

Arabic Language School (CVC)

Islamic School (CVC)

Science & Technology club (Blue School)

Community events (Eid, Iftaar, Ladies Night etc.) - Community event food expenses

- Teachers salaries

- School books and stationery

We also have a separate bank account for the Cambourne Mosque Project.
