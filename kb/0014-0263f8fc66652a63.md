# Arabic Teacher required - Cambourne Crescent

With the growing interest in learning Classical Arabic, we are looking for a teacher who can teach Fus’ha for adults.

Please fill in the form if you think this would be of interest to you –

- The classes will run in Cambourne

- Classes are expected to start in September/October 2018

- Classes will be either on Sundays or on weekday evenings

- Classes are expected to be of 2-hours once a week

- Students will be mainly non-Arabic speaking adults

- The teacher would be expected to be fluent in Arabic and English

- Remuneration for the teacher is available.
