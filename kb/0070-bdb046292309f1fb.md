# Science workshop at The Cambridge Stem Cell Institute - Cambourne Crescent

Students from our Islamic School attend a science workshop at The Cambridge Stem Cell Institute, a world-leading centre for stem cell research.

Their mission is to transform human health through a deep understanding of stem cell biology, funded by Wellcome–MRC Cambridge Stem Cell Institute
