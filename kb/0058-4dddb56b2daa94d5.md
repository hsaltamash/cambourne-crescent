# Arabic Language School (for adults) - Cambourne Crescent

We plan to have the Arabic Language Classes (for adults) in CVC on Sunday from 10:00-12:00.

The classes are for non-Arabic Speakers, and are usually informal, more of conversation and discussions, but extremely focused. Students will be taught how to read, write and speak modern standard Arabic. We aim to teach purely classic Arabic Fusha to the students.

There will be a small fee to cover the booking cost of the venue. There is an upper limit on number of students, if anyone wants to join in, please contact us.
