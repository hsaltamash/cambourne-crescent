# 7 Good Deeds You Can Do with Snowfall - Cambourne Crescent

We are encouraged to live a life of Ihsaan (excellence), making the most of every opportunity to perform good deeds, no matter how small.

The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said, “Do not belittle any good deed, even meeting your brother (Muslim) with a cheerful face”.

[Riyad as-Salihin 121]

With winter in full swing and snowfall becoming a common sight in many parts of the world, here are seven simple yet impactful good deeds you can do when it snows.

1. Thank Allah

- Begin by expressing gratitude to Allah for protecting you from harsh weather conditions.

- Thank Him for blessing you with a warm home, heating, and suitable clothing to stay comfortable during the cold months.

2. Pray for the Less Fortunate

- Remember those who are homeless or cannot afford warm clothes and blankets in your prayers.

- Pray for their safety and well-being during these challenging winter months.

3. Be Kind to Your Neighbour

- If you’re clearing snow from your car, take a moment to remove snow from your neighbour’s car as well.

- Small acts of kindness can make a big difference in fostering good relationships.

4. Clear the Ice

- Snow that melts often turns into ice, making walkways slippery and hazardous.

- Use a broom to clear ice from pavements near your home and your neighbours’ homes. This simple task can prevent accidents.

5. Check on Elderly Neighbours

- Check with elderly neighbours to ensure their heating is working and that they are warm.

- Offer to help with their groceries or other errands, as it may be difficult for them to go out in snowy conditions.

6. Help the Homeless

- If you know of homeless individuals in your area, provide them with a warm blanket or a hot drink.

- Share information about local homeless shelters where they can find refuge during the cold weather.

7. Bring Joy to Your Family

- Spend quality time with your family by having a snowball fight.

- Do this with the intention of spreading joy and creating happy memories for your loved ones.

Snowfall provides unique opportunities to perform acts of kindness and bring warmth to others. Let’s embrace these moments to make a positive impact on those around us while earning rewards from Allah SWT.
