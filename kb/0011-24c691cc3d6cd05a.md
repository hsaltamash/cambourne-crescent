# Food for Jimmy's Shelter - Cambourne Crescent

Saturday 2nd June we are cooking food for Jimmy’s Shelter for Homeless.

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, “Feed the hungry, visit the sick, and set free the captives.”

[Sahih al-Bukhari 5649]

On average Jimmy’s Shelter feeds 35 people (homeless and few caretakers). For over 20 years, Jimmy’s has been providing help for the homeless in Cambridge.

Ramadan is a time for increased charity and generosity. Fasting is intended to bring Muslims closer to God and to remind them of those in the world who are hungry and less fortunate.

We will be providing the following menu:

- Curry (any)

- Pulao/Rice/Biryani

- Kebabs

- Naan Breads

- Sweet/Dessert

Please let us know if you wish to help with cooking/delivery etc.
