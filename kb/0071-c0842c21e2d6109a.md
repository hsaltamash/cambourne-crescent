# Clothes Donation - Cambourne Crescent

Got any old clothes 👕🧣🧦 you no longer wear but haven’t thrown out yet? Well, why not put them to good use and help save lives!

We will be collecting any unwanted items in Cambourne on Saturday 12th of November at our Isha venue and then take them to Cambridge Central Mosque.

The items you donate will be sold to fund lifesaving projects all around the world!

🗓 Saturday 12th November

🕐 7:30 – 8 pm
