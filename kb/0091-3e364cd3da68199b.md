# Back to School Meetup - Cambourne Crescent

Saturday, 14th Sept, 6:00 pm

Cambourne HUB

We will In’sha Allah have,

- Bring & Share – One Dish

- family get-together

- Seerah Competition.

We kindly ask you to bring a main dish and, if you’d like, a dessert to share. Please ensure that your food is ready to serve and includes any necessary serving utensils. To accommodate everyone, all food must be certified Halal. If you’re purchasing from a store, such as for pizza, please ensure it’s from a place that exclusively prepares and serves Halal food. Pizzas will be provided for all Crescent School students and other children, courtesy of The Grill Peri Peri in Cambridge.
