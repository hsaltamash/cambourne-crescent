# Islamic School Teacher Vacancy - Cambourne Crescent

The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said:

“When a man dies, his deeds come to an end except for three things: Sadaqah Jariyah (ceaseless charity); knowledge which is beneficial; or a virtuous descendant who prays for him (the deceased).”

[Sahih Muslim]

Due to continued expansion and changing work patterns of the existing team of teachers, we now require 2 additional teaching staff for Islamic School.

The Islamic school runs twice a week, every Monday and Wednesday from 6 – 7.30pm.

It may seem daunting at first, but the power of education to transform lives is hugely rewarding and we sincerely hope you will be able to offer your services in some capacity either one or two evening per week. Full training and backup support will be provided.

Initially, you may just want to observe a class for a few weeks and then make a decision, or you may have previous teaching experience, either way, please do get in touch for further information by sending an email to info@cambournecrescent.org
