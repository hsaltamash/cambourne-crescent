# Islamic School Teacher Vacancy - Cambourne Crescent

The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said:

“The best among you (Muslims) are those who learn the Qur’an and teach it.”

[Sahih al-Bukhari]

- The Islamic school runs twice a week, every Monday and Wednesday from 6 – 7.30 pm.

- Teachers may choose to take Monday or Wednesday class, depending on their availability.

- Experience Certificates to all teachers and staff is provided (which could be used as work experience).

- Remuneration for the teacher is available.

Please do get in touch for further information by sending an email to info@cambournecrescent.org
