# EID AL FITR 1446 / 2025 - Cambourne Crescent

Eid Day

Eid Salah will be held at the Cricket Ground this year, In’sha Allah. Please arrive by 9:30 AM, as Jama’at will begin promptly at 10:00 AM.

Clocks go forward! This will affect your alarms and prayer times, so please adjust accordingly.

Venue: New Cricket Pavilion (NCP) – map

Date: Sunday, 30th 2025

Time:

9:30 – Arrival & Takbeerat ul Eid

10:00 – Eid Jama’at

10:30–11:30 – Picnic

Please,

- Arrive Early – Salah starts promptly, and the Khutbah follows after the prayer (unlike Jumu’ah).

- Bring a Prayer Mat – Bring prayer mat, and a waterproof sheet in case the grass is damp.

- Picnic Essentials – Feel free to bring your own picnic, along with a blanket, rug, or chair.

- Transport Considerations – Walk, cycle, or car share whenever possible, as parking is very limited. Please avoid street parking.

- Alternative Parking – If needed, use parking at the HUB and Blue School.

Alternate Venue & time

If it rains, we will hold two indoor Salah at the HUB instead.

- 6:30 – Arrival & Takbeerat ul Eid, 7:00 – Jam’ah

- 7:30 – Arrival & Takbeerat ul Eid, 8:00 – Jam’ah
