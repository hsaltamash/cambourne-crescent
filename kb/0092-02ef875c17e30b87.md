# Cambourne VTF – Thank you Taylor Wimpey - Cambourne Crescent

Thank you, Taylor Wimpey, many thanks for reaching out following our plea for a donation to Cambourne’s Volunteer Task Force (in response to COVID-19).

Your help comes at a perfect time when we have started to see increasing demand for our service in the Cambourne community.

We are delivering more than 50 food parcels every week in and around Cambourne. Your help will allow us to progress quickly and efficiently in helping the vulnerable, the older and the isolated people in our community by providing food parcels and many other essential items.
