# EID AL ADHA 1445 / 2024 - Cambourne Crescent

Eid Day

We will be praying the Eid Salah in Cricket ground this year, In’sha Allah. Please be at the venue by 9 am as Jama’at will be at 9.30 am prompt.

Venue: New Cricket Pavilion (NCP) – map

Date: Sunday, 16th June 2024

Time:

9:00 – Arrival & Takbeerat ul Eid

9:30 – Eid Jama’at

10:00–11:30 – Picnic

Please,

- plan to Arrive early as the salat starts promptly and the khutbah comes after the salat (not like Jum’a)

- do bring prayer mats

- feel free to bring your own picnic, and also picnic blanket/rug/chair.

- walk, cycle or car share. Since parking space is very limited, we do encourage families to walk or car share. Please avoid using street parking.

- if needed, please use parking at the HUB and Blue School.

Alternate Venue & time

For those who are concerned about the rain are welcome to join the indoor salah at the HUB

7:30 Arrival & Takbeerat ul Eid

8:00 Jam’ah
