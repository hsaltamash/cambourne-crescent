# Cambourne Great Common Farm - Cambourne Crescent

A Beacon of Faith, Community, and Service

The Cambourne Mosque Project is more than just a building; it’s a dream to create a welcoming sanctuary and a vibrant hub that serves the diverse needs of our growing community. We aim to establish a calm oasis for reflection, learning, and connection for everyone in Cambourne and beyond, fostering peace and understanding.

Our Vision: A Multi-Functional Space for All

Prayer Facility

A serene main hall (approx. 45m x 15m) for daily and congregational prayers, accommodating up to 400 worshippers, with segregated areas. Designed for tranquility and spiritual reflection.

Community & Event Hall

A versatile space for community gatherings, cultural events, workshops, and social activities, fostering unity and engagement across all backgrounds.

Educational Classrooms

8-10 dedicated classrooms for diverse educational programs including Arabic, Maths, and English, alongside youth clubs and skills development workshops.

Community Coffee Shop

A welcoming coffee shop (approx. 15m x 10m) with indoor/outdoor seating, serving as a social hub, potential food bank, and meeting space for the wider community.

Open Garden & Allotment

A tranquil green space for relaxation, social interaction, and community gardening. Plans include growing produce for a community soup kitchen and exploring beekeeping.

Ample & Eco-Friendly Parking

Parking for 70-100 cars, thoughtfully designed to minimize environmental impact and ease traffic congestion, including support for The Vine School pick-ups/drop-offs.

Additional facilities will include an office, storage, and adaptable spaces for indoor/outdoor club activities, all designed with eco-friendliness, sustainability (solar panels, rainwater harvesting), and accessibility at their core.

Alhamdulillah, we have successfully purchased the 1.6-acre plot of land (approx. 6500 m²) at Great Common Farm for £280,445.20. This is a monumental first step, secured by your trust and generosity!

Current Focus:

- Preparing and submitting the Pre-Planning Application to the local authorities.
