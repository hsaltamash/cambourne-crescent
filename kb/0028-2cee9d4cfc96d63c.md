# Feed the Homeless this Ramadan - Cambourne Crescent

We have pledged with Jimmy’s Shelter Cambridge to provide Home Cooked meal for Sunday 26th May InShaAllah.

Ramadan is a time for increased charity and generosity. Fasting is intended to bring Muslims closer to God and to remind them of those in the world who are hungry and less fortunate.

The Prophet (ﷺ) said,

“Feed the hungry, visit the sick, and set free the captives.”

We need help from families to cook for about 35 people. We are looking for help with preparation of food.

Please get in touch if you are able to help with cooking/delivery etc.

Please share the warmth with those who need it most.
