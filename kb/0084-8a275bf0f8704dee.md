# Salah Challenge - Cambourne Crescent

We’ve been blessed to witness many children regularly attending prayers with their fathers. Their dedication is truly inspiring!

We are excited to announce that we will have the Salah challenge prize distribution after Isha prayer.

Join us on Sunday evening for a joyous occasion.

HUB Hall

Sunday 3rd December, 8 – 10 pm

Tea/Coffee, delicious cakes & delightful desserts will be provided, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for our community.

May Allah shower His blessings upon our children for their commitment to prayers and grant them abundant rewards. May He also reward the parents for their dedication to supporting their children on this beautiful journey.

Looking forward to celebrating the achievements of our young ones, In’sha Allah
