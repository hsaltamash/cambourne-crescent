# Local Community Members Launch Innovative Salah Notification Apps - Cambourne Crescent

Two talented members of the community, Shakeel Ahmed and Zain Imran, have developed apps designed to keep users informed about Salah times and their respective venues. This initiative aims to strengthen the bond among the community and enhance convenience in attending congregational prayers.

- The Apple app: Shakeel Ahmed has successfully developed an iOS app, now available on the Apple App Store. The app offers real-time Salah notifications, complete with venue details, ensuring that users never miss a congregational prayer.

- The Android app: Zain Imran, a school student, has taken the lead in developing the Android version which is currently in its beta stage. Despite his young age, Zain has demonstrated exceptional talent and initiative in serving the community through this innovative project.

The community is encouraged to make du’a for these brothers and their families, recognizing their sincere efforts and dedication to serving the ummah.
