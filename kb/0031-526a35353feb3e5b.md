# Eid al-Adha Prayer 2019 - Cambourne Crescent

Venue: Cambourne HUB, CB23 6GW – map

Date: Sunday 11th August 2019

Time:

7:45 am – Takbeerat ul Eid

8:15 am – Eid Jama’at

Everyone to be in the hall by 8.00 am as Jama’at will be held at 8.15 am prompt.

It was narrated that Ibn ‘Umar(RA) said:“The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) used to go out to the ‘Eid prayers walking, and come back walking.”

Sunan Ibn Majah

Although some parking is available, we do encourage families to car share or walk. Kindly remember to bring a prayer mat.
