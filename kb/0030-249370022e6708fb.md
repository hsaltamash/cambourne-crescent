# Eid al-Fitr Prayer 2019 - Cambourne Crescent

Venue: Cambourne HUB, CB23 6GW – map

Date: Tuesday 4th June 2019

Time:

7:30 am – Takbeerat ul Eid

8:00 am – Eid Jama’at

Everyone to be in the hall by 7.50 am as Jama’at will be held at 8.00 am prompt.

Narrated Ibn `Umar(RA): The Prophet (ﷺ) ordered the people to pay Zakat-ul-Fitr before going to the `Id prayer.

Sahih al-Bukhari Book 24, Hadith 110

Zakat ul Fitr is set at £5 per person, this is due before the Eid prayer. There will be collection boxes at the venue.

It was narrated that Ibn ‘Umar(RA) said:“The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) used to go out to the ‘Eid prayers walking, and come back walking.”

Sunan Ibn Majah

Although some parking is available, we do encourage families to car share or walk. Kindly remember to bring a prayer mat.

Cambourne Family Fete (Eid celebration) has been arranged for Saturday 8th June (1 to 6 pm) at CVC.
